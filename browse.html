Browsing REST Endpoints

One of the best things about Esri's REST endpoints (and presumably others - got any examples?) is how you can explore them with your browser just by clicking links. At the "top" of a server you'll find a list of all the services (of type Map, Feature, Geocode, etc) and if you click into those services, you'll see the layers and tables available. At the bottom of each service you'll see the supported functions. Click a layer or table and you'll see the columns available. At the bottom of those pages you'll find links to forms like Query, where you can interactively build a query with a form.
